Card Games Rules


There are 52 cards in a standard deck that are divided into 26 red and 26 black cards.

There are 4 of each type of card: Ace, Spades, Kings, Queens, Jacks

There are numbers on the cards ranging from 2-10

The flush is the set of cards with the same suit

Suit: A type of card for example a jack
